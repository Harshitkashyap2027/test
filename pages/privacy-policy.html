Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 23, 2026.

lvlBase uses Firebase services to provide secure authentication, database, storage, and analytics for schools and organizations.

Data we collect

  • Account details and role metadata.
  • Operational records (attendance, assignments, grades, communication events).
  • System usage metrics for reliability and performance improvements.

How we use data

  • Deliver product functionality and role-specific dashboards.
  • Provide support and audit logs.
  • Improve service stability and user experience.

Security

All access is governed by role-based permissions and secure transport. Firestore rules and authentication policies must be configured by each deployment admin.
