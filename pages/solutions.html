Module: Healthcare Matrix

The Intelligent Hospital

HIPAA compliant EHR, predictive triage, and seamless shift management.

Core Features

Unified EHR

Electronic Health Records that load instantly, with a visual timeline of a patient's entire medical history.

AI Shift & Bed Routing

The system predicts ER surges and automatically alerts on-call staff and reallocates ward beds.

Pharmacy & Inventory Sync

Real-time tracking of medicine stocks. Auto-generates purchase orders when critical supplies drop below safe levels.

Zero-Knowledge Security

Patient data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Only authorized personnel assigned to the patient can decrypt the chart.
